KTID: Knowledge Supporting Tool For Human-Computer Interaction Design

KTID, a tool that aims to support the capture and sharing of useful knowledge to assist in HCI design. Supports knowledge management (capture, representation, storage, retrieval, use and evaluation) to aid in HCI design of interactive systems. It allows HCI designers to annotate structured information about design choices into design artifacts and share it with other HCI design stakeholders.

Undergraduate Project

KTID was developed in the context of a master research (Castro, 2021) and an undergraduate project (Sessa, 2021) and uses a fragment of HCI-ON, which includes concepts from HCI Design Ontology (HCIDO), HCIO and some ontologies of SEON.

The undergraduate project report is available (in Portuguese only) at (Manso, 2022).

The master thesis is available at (Castro, 2021).
